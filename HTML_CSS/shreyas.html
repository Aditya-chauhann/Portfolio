About Me

I am a filmmaker currently pursuing formal education in filmmaking, building on a foundation of design studies. Since 2017, I have honed my skills in video editing, working on numerous selfproduced music videos, short films, and documentaries. My diverse portfolio includes a variety of projects, from commercials to photoshoots. I bring a multifaceted skill set to every project, functioning as a photographer, director, director of photography (DoP), and editor. My work has been featured by artists like Prabh Deep, Yashraj, Bharg among many other artists from the Indian indie/hip hop music space
